Vision and Mission


Knackture is a unique platform for artists to utilise their talent fully and excel in their chosen field of interest. The virtusos will be able to engage with experts, who will help them gauge their performance in the a particular field and guide them towards their dream career.

Collaborations


Creative collaborations in the field of art is a great way to build relationships thereby utilising multiple avenues of access. Networking a considerable way to make new friends and take advantage of possible gig.Sometimes it can be much easier to reach out to people online. Our website will help with networking and enable them to make use of every opportunity for promising artists by saving their time and avoiding certain pitfalls or failures.

Social Media


An easy way for artists to establish their talent /domain is with the help of social media. This includes sharing relevant posts. Using social media is also one of the most cost effective methods for showcasing right talent. Creating an account and uploading a profile is free of cost. Another great advantage is that social media enables you to use hashtags and follow others to get a toehold becoming popular and get recognition. It is still faster than print advertisements or online job portals.
